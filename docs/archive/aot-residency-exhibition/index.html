Ben Moren

The work revolves around two diverse thematic ideas, education and video game culture. While exploring how these ideas play off of and into each other, two main works were created. A series of drawings by Matthew and a projection and controller installation by Ben Moren. Lawler's drawings explore anomalies in space and are presented in a way that breaks the human idea of perspective. Moren's educational video game interface, which is modeled on a classic school desk, allows users to navigate a three dimensional recreation of the gallery space in real time where virtual versions of Lawler's drawings stare there physical versions on the opposing wall. The virtual versions have the perspective Lawler's drawings break corrected digitally. Doritos and Mountain Dew were served at the opening.

A collaborative project between Matthew Lawler and Myself for the Art of This SUMMER open Residency program.
